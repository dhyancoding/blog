PLACES YOU MUST VISIT!

prithvi cafe

I have visited this place in december 2021 and it is an amazing place!

It has amazing coffee and is a great chill out place for friends! I am sure we will be coming back to this place on our next visit to India and recommend "Prithvi Cafe" to everyone who likes good food. (The only warning about this place is the portions are huge and the food is addictive :)

jantar mantar, jaipur

I visited this place way back in 2017 August and it was spectacular!

Jantar mantar is the place of collection of architectural astronomical instruments. Which is buit by king Sawi Jai Singh. Jantar Mantar in Jaipur is the largest of five astronomical observatories built by Maharaja Jai Singh during the period 1727-1734.
